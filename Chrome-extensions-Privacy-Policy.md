## 웨않돼 - 한국어 오타 자동수정

- "웨않돼 - 한국어 오타 자동수정" stores only data closely related to its functionality.
- All data are stored offline in your browser.
- No data are collected from your browser.
- No data are transmitted to anywhere, ever.
